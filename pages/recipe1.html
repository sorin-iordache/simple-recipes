Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients:

  • 200 g spaghetti
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 50 ml olive oil
  • Red chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parmesan (optional)
  • Fresh parsley

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted water according to the package instructions.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large pan and add the thinly sliced garlic. Saut√© until golden brown (about 2-3 minutes).
  3. Add red chili flakes if desired.
  4. Drain the pasta, reserving some pasta water.
  5. Add pasta to the pan and toss well; add pasta water if it seems dry.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and parsley. Serve with grated Parmesan if desired.
