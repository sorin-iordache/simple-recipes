Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients:

  • 200 g spaghetti
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 50 ml olive oil
  • Red chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parmesan (optional)
  • Fresh parsley

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted water according to the package instructions.
  2. In the meantime, heat the olive oil in a large pan and add the thinly sliced garlic. Sauté until golden brown (about 2-3 minutes).
  3. If you like it spicy, add red chili flakes.
  4. Once the pasta is ready, drain it, reserving a little pasta water.
  5. Add the pasta to the pan and toss well. If it's too dry, add a bit of the reserved pasta water.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and chopped parsley. Serve with grated Parmesan if desired.

Vegetable Omelette

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 tomato
  • 50 g cheese (optional)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl, adding salt and pepper.
  2. Dice the vegetables (onion, bell pepper, tomato) into small pieces.
  3. In a pan with a little olive oil, sauté the onion and bell pepper for 3-4 minutes, then add the tomatoes and cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. Pour the beaten eggs over the veggies and let cook on low heat. You can add cheese before the eggs fully set if you'd like.
  5. Serve the omelette warm, either on its own or with a side salad.

Chicken and Avocado Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (preferably whole wheat)
  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 avocado
  • Lettuce
  • Yogurt sauce or light mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil

  1. Grill or pan-cook the chicken breast with a little olive oil, seasoning with salt and pepper.
  2. While the chicken cooks, slice the avocado and remove the pit.
  3. Spread a thin layer of yogurt sauce or light mayonnaise on each slice of bread.
  4. Place the sliced chicken breast on one slice of bread, followed by avocado slices and a few lettuce leaves.
  5. Top with the other slice of bread and serve immediately
